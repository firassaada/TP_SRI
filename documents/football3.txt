### Handout 3: The Role of Tactics in Football

Tactics play a crucial role in football, dictating how teams organize and play on the pitch. Coaches develop formations and strategies to exploit their opponents' weaknesses and capitalize on their own teams' strengths. Common formations include the 4-4-2, 4-3-3, and 3-5-2, each with their own advantages and disadvantages. Good tactics can make the difference between victory and defeat, making the role of the coach just as crucial as that of the players on the pitch.
